We’re building a world where access matters more than ownership.

Buying a camera, console, or laptop for a one-time need doesn’t always make sense. With Shiftty, you can rent electronics from people in your community — and let your own devices earn money when you’re not using them.

It’s simple: better access to tech, less waste, and more money in people’s pockets.

How it works

List your tech

Snap a few photos, set your price, and make your devices available for rent.

Get booked

People nearby discover your items and book them for the days they need.

Earn money

Secure payments, deposits, and photo proof keep every rental safe and simple.
