Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2025

Shiftty (“we”, “our”, “us”) operates the website shiftty-launch.com. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you join our waitlist:

  • Email address
  • Your consent to receive updates

Once the full Shiftty platform launches, we may collect additional information such as name, verification documents, listings, and rental history. This will be updated accordingly.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used for:

  • Sending early access notifications
  • Product updates and announcements
  • Improving our marketing and product planning

We do not sell or share your data with third parties for advertising.

3. Legal Basis (GDPR)

Under EU law, we process your data based on:

  • Consent — when you subscribe to our waitlist
  • Legitimate interest — to improve our product and communication

4. Data Storage

Your email is securely stored through Formspree and Shiftty’s internal systems. We retain your data until you unsubscribe or request deletion.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data
  • Request deletion
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • Request correction
  • Export your data

To exercise these rights, email us at: privacy@shiftty-launch.com

6. Third-Party Services

We use:

  • Formspree — for collecting email signups
  • Cloudflare — for DNS, security, and performance
We ensure these providers comply with GDPR regulations.

7. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at: privacy@shiftty-launch.com
