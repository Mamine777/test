This Cookie Policy explains how Shiftty uses cookies on shiftty-launch.com.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device to allow websites to function properly and improve your experience.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

Required for the site to function (security, load balancing, waitlist form operation).

Analytics Cookies (Optional)

We currently do not use analytics cookies. If added later, users will be notified and asked for consent.

3. Why We Use Cookies

  • Improve website performance
  • Maintain security and reliability
  • Remember user consent preferences

4. Cookie Consent

When you first visit our site, you will see a cookie banner asking you to accept cookies. You may reject cookies by adjusting your browser settings.

5. Managing Cookies

You can delete or block cookies via your browser settings (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge).
